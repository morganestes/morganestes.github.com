About Me

I’m a front-end web developer based in the Oklahoma City metro area. I mostly work in PHP, HTML, CSS, jQuery, and MySQL, but am always open to learning and using new tools. Lately I’ve been working on expanding into CoffeeScript, Node.js, and responsive design.

I’m a big fan of the semantic web, typography, and the fun that HTML5 brings to the table (but not table layouts).

I dabble in digital photography, and even had one of my pictures published in the Oklahoma Bar Journal.
